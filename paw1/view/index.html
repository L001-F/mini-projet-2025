About Computer Science Department

Welcome to a hub of innovation and excellence! Our Computer Science Department offers a dynamic environment where theory meets practical application. We provide comprehensive programs in software engineering, web development, AI, cybersecurity, and more. With a strong focus on hands-on learning, collaborative projects, and cutting-edge research, we empower students to turn ideas into impactful solutions.

Our passionate faculty and vibrant community foster creativity, critical thinking, and professional growth. Whether you're aspiring to be a developer, data scientist, or tech leader, our department provides the tools and knowledge to shape your future in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Our Specialties

Information (LMD)

This program focuses on developing strong foundations in Information Systems. Students gain expertise in managing IT infrastructure, analyzing systems, and understanding how to optimize organizational processes using modern technologies.

Software Engineering

In this specialty, students develop expertise in software design, development, and maintenance. The program offers a strong understanding of programming languages, algorithms, and the principles of building scalable and efficient software solutions.

Frequently Asked Questions

To access the student portal, you must first register using your student ID and official email address. Once registered, you can log in at any time using your credentials to view important academic and administrative updates.

To view your profile information, log in to the portal and navigate to the "Profile" section. Here, you can check your personal details such as contact information and address. Please ensure your information is correct and up to date.

If your document request is rejected, check the reason for rejection in the "My Requests" section. Common reasons include incomplete information or missing attachments. Update the required details and resubmit your request.

To track the status of your document request, log in to the portal and navigate to the "My Requests" section. Here, you will see a list of your submitted requests with their current statuses, such as "Pending," "In Progress," or "Ready for Pickup."

If you need further assistance or have additional questions, please fill out the form available in the "Contact Us" section of this website. Our support team will get back to you promptly with the information you need.
